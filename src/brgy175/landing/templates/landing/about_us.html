HISTORY


Barangay 175 was created by virtue of 1972 Constitution. Geographically, Barangay 175 bounded by Bagong Silang (Barangay 176) in the North, Cielito (Barangay 177) in the south, Area B Camarin (Barangay 174) in the East and Bagumbong (Barangay 171) in the West. It has a total land Area of 275 hectares, with an approximate population.

The first appointed Barangay Captain was the late Felipe Samano, Mr. Benigno Dela Cruz was the first elected Barangay Captain in 1982. Mr. Benigno Dela Cruz was re-elected in 1989 under Batas Pambansa 337, otherwise known as the Local Government Code of 1989. Mr. Romeo Bantugan was elected in 1994 under the local government code of 1991 then re-elected in 1997, Barangay Captain Alfredo Estrello was elected as Punong Barangay on July 15, 2002, and Mr. Ireneo Cayetano seated Ruben Z. Dela Cruz administered the Barangay since February 2009 up to the present.

ABOUT

Barangay 175- Zone 15 – District 1 is the fourth largest barangay in the entire City of Caloocan in terms of land area, annual income and population. It is situated in Camarin, within the so-called “bukid” area of Caloocan. Barangay 175 approximately 18 kilometers away from the City proper and is now considered as a developed community.
